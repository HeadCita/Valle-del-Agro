The Enterprise

CORPORACIÓN VALLE DEL AGRO sac. is a Peruvian enterprise dedicated to the production, commercialization of vegetables and fresh products in the local and internationals markets. As agro industrial enterprise, we are focused on give products of quality to our clients and their families; we are interested in the well-being of each integrant and we select each fruit with high standards of quality in each process.

Vision

In 2023, to be recognized as one of the main agro industrial enterprises of our country, to be a producer and an exporter of high quality products certified internationally.

Mission

To be recognized at the regional macro level for our agro industrial products of first quality; generating a chain of values with our employees, suppliers, and clients; contributing to the environment and our community.

Values

QUALITY
of the products and services.


EFFECTIVENESS and EFFICIENCY
in all the chain of supplies.


INTEGRATION and IDENTIFICATION
of the participants and allies in our organization.
